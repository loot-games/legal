# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 16, 2026**

"Drilloot" (the "App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

- **Advertising Identifier (IDFA)**: Collected via Google AdMob only when you grant permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt. Used for ad personalization and delivery optimization.
- **Device Information**: Anonymized device information (OS version, device model, language settings) collected by Google AdMob for ad delivery.
- **Crash Reports**: Anonymous crash information may be sent via Apple's systems if you allow it. This is managed by Apple, not by us.

The App does not require user accounts and does not collect any personally identifiable information such as name, email, or location. All game progress is stored locally on your device.

## 2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely for:

- Displaying and optimizing advertisements
- Investigating bugs and improving app quality

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

- **Google AdMob** (advertising): [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

Google AdMob collects and uses information in accordance with its own privacy policy.

## 4. Your Choices

You can control tracking through the following methods:

- **Disable ATT**: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → turn off permission for this app
- **Reset Advertising ID**: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Reset Advertising Identifier

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ but we do not recommend its use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## 6. International Data Transfers

Data collected by Google AdMob may be transferred to servers in various countries, including the United States.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top.
