# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 23, 2026**

"steeroot" (the "App") respects your privacy. The App **does not collect any personal information or usage data**.

## 1. Information We Collect

**The App does not make network requests and does not collect any information about you.**

- No user accounts
- No advertising
- No analytics
- No in-app purchases

All game progress (cleared stages, settings) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted.

## 2. Information Managed by Apple

Independently of this App, Apple may collect the following with your permission:

- **Crash Reports**: Anonymous crash information may be sent to Apple if enabled in iOS settings. The App developer only sees aggregated reports provided by Apple.

## 3. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and designed for all ages. Because no personal information is collected, there are no concerns regarding children's privacy.

## 4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top.
